Looking for more inspiration? These sites are treasure troves of text-mode art history, preserving the entire legacy of the text-mode art scene:
  • 16colo.rs: An interactive gallery showcasing every art pack released since 1990.
  • aSCII aRENA: A web-based BBS for exploring and sharing ASCII art and amiga "collys."
  • Artpacks.org: A simple depot of zipped scene releases organized by year.
  • ArtScene Textfiles: Historical archives and classic tools for ANSI art.
